About ApplePicker.CO

Who are we?

ApplePicker.co is a platform dedicated to providing "The Apple" to HOMELESS people in America.

Why do we do this?

We believe that everyone deserves access to fresh and nutritious food, regardless of their circumstances. By providing apples to homeless individuals, we aim to make a positive impact on their health and well-being.

How can you help?

You can support our mission by donating apples, volunteering your time, or spreading the word about our cause. Every little bit helps! Plus you can help us pick which "The Apple" to give.

Q&A
Q: Where do the apples come from?
A: We source our apples from local farms and orchards that prioritize sustainable and ethical farming practices.
Q: How are the apples distributed?
A: We work with local shelters and outreach programs to ensure that the apples reach those who need them most.
